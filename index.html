About Btech Energi

Pioneering Clean Energy Across Nigeria

Founded with a mission to accelerate Nigeria's renewable energy transition, Btech Energi has been at the forefront of solar technology since our inception. We design, install, and maintain solar energy systems that are built to last.

From residential rooftop panels to large-scale commercial installations, our team of certified engineers delivers turnkey solutions tailored to your energy needs. We believe in sustainable growth — for our clients and for the planet.
